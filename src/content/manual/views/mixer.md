The Mixer view shows every track as a horizontal channel strip with a fader, pan control, mute and solo buttons, and a level meter. Open it from the **Mixer** button in the footer while working in any main editing view.

Each strip displays the track header with name, color, and the same context actions available elsewhere: stop track output, change color, reorder, duplicate, or delete. Engaging solo on one track mutes others for monitoring.

Use the mixer for balancing levels while arranging or performing, not for deep sound design—that belongs in Clip Detail's device panels. Add **MIDI** or **Audio** tracks from the column at the end of the strip row, matching the launcher’s track-creation workflow.
